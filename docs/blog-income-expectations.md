## What Matters Most

1. **Consistency** - Post regularly (2-4x per month minimum)
2. **SEO** - Wait for Google to index, monitor Search Console
3. **Traffic quality** - Pinterest + organic search best for affiliate conversions
4. **Patience** - Most blogs take 6-12 months to gain traction
5. **Content quantity** - Target 50-100 posts for serious traffic

---

## Key Takeaways

- Do not expect significant income for 6-12 months
- Focus on building content and traffic first
- Most bloggers who quit do so before month 6
- Those who succeed are those who keep going
- Diversify income (AdSense + affiliate + eventually products)
