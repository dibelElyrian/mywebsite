WFH setups remain popular, and the best upgrades focus on **comfort and long-term use**. These essentials solve daily pain points without blowing the budget.

## Essentials worth prioritizing

- **Ergonomic chair**: prioritize lumbar support and breathable fabric.
- **Desk lamp**: a warm LED lamp reduces eye strain at night.
- **Laptop stand**: improves posture and airflow for the device.
- **Affordable mic or headset**: keeps meetings clear and professional.

## Smart buying reminders

- Measure your workspace before ordering.
- Check for local warranty or easy return policies.
- Look for bundled accessories to stretch your budget.
