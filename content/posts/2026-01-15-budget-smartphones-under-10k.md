Budget smartphone demand keeps rising. Shoppers focus on **battery life, camera quality, and storage**. Here are the key checks before you buy.

## What to prioritize under ₱10K

- **Battery**: 5,000mAh or higher is the sweet spot.
- **Storage**: 128GB is ideal if you store lots of photos and apps.
- **Display**: A 90Hz screen feels smoother for scrolling.

## Key checks before checkout

- Confirm LTE/5G compatibility with your carrier.
- Compare bundled freebies such as cases or screen protectors.
- Read the most recent reviews for real-world performance notes.

*Prices and availability may change. Always verify specs from the official listing.*
