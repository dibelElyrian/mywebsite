## What makes a kitchen item sulit?

- **Durability per peso**: look for stainless steel or heat-resistant materials.
- **Compact storage**: stackable or foldable designs save space.
- **Easy cleanup**: dishwasher-safe or non-stick surfaces save time.

4. **Non-stick frying pan**. Versatile for daily meals with less oil.
