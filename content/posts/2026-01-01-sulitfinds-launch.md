SulitFinds is a simple guide for **budget-friendly product recommendations** in the Philippines. We focus on practical picks, clear summaries, and easy-to-follow buying guides that help you shop smarter without overspending.

## What you can expect

- **Curated lists** of sulit picks for everyday needs.
- **Buying guides** that explain what to look for before you check out.
- **Price-sensitive tips** so you can compare options quickly.

## How we choose recommendations

We review product listings, specifications, reviews, and community feedback to identify items that offer good value. We do **not** claim personal testing, and we keep recommendations fair and easy to verify.
