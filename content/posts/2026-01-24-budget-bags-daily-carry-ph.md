## Start with your daily carry list

Write down what you bring every day. Most people carry a phone, wallet, keys, power bank, and a small pouch. Your list tells you the minimum bag size you need and whether you need room for items like a bottle or a compact lunch container.

## Size and shape that match your routine

A bag that is too large feels heavy and gets in the way. A bag that is too small becomes cluttered and hard to use.

- **Compact sling or chest bag** for phone, wallet, keys, and a small power bank.
- **Medium sling or shoulder bag** for a small tablet, makeup kit, or folded umbrella.
- **Small tote** if you carry a lunch bag or light office items.

For quick errands, a compact sling is easier to carry and safer in crowded places.

## Material, straps, and closures

Budget bags can last if you choose the right basics.

- **Material:** Canvas is light and easy to clean. PU or faux leather looks neat but needs gentle care. Nylon or coated fabric handles light rain better.
- **Straps:** Wide straps are easier for heavier loads. Thin straps work for light carry only.
- **Closures:** Zippers are safer for commuting than magnetic buttons.

## Pocket layout and comfort

Pocket layout affects how fast you can reach essentials. Aim for at least one inner pocket for coins or small items and a front pocket for transit cards. Check size charts or review photos so the bag depth matches what you carry.

## Quick decision checklist

- If you carry only essentials, a compact sling or chest bag is enough.
- If you bring lunch or a notebook, a small tote is more practical.
- For rainy months, choose coated fabric or a bag with a water resistance note.

## Quick FAQ

- **Question:** What size sling bag is enough for daily commuting?

  **Answer:** A sling bag that fits your phone, wallet, keys, and a small power bank is enough for most commutes. Check the width and depth, not just the length.

- **Question:** Is PU leather good for budget bags?

  **Answer:** PU leather is fine for light daily use if you keep it dry and wipe it clean. It is not as durable as real leather but can still last with careful use.

- **Question:** Do I need a waterproof bag for Manila rain?

  **Answer:** It helps during sudden showers, but most budget bags are only splash resistant. Use a small rain cover or keep a plastic pouch inside for extra protection.
